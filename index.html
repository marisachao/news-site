As the Warriors continue to pile on the wins this season, people have begun to wonder whether it is possible for the team to beat the record for wins in a season that was set by Michael Jordan's Chicago Bulls during the 1995-1996 season and has seemed unbreakable ever since. Even with Andre Iguodala's injury, the Warriors look like they are well positioned to break the record this year. Stephen Curry isn't just the MVP - he is revolutionizing the game
